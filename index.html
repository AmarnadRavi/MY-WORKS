DESCRIPTION

Creative Hut Institute of Photography, Kottayam is an exclusive Photography College that imparts photography education in a practical manner so that students can have all the expertise for a better future career aspect in Photography & Cinematography. Creative Hut Institute of Photography was established in 2007 in Kerala. Creative Hut is known for providing the best photography knowledge.Creative Hut Institute of Photography Professional Diploma in Photography program is offered in several specializations, namely, Art Photography, Wildlife Photography, Fashion Photography, Product Photography, Travel Photography & Photojournalism. For admission to any of the courses, students should have at least a 10+2 level pass or SSLC. This pedagogical institute keeps its application process in an Online mode.
